# Biochemistry

Biochemistry covers the molecular and metabolic basis of human biology — from enzyme kinetics and metabolic pathways to molecular biology and inborn errors — contributing ~11 questions per NEET PG paper.

## Files in This Folder

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|

| `biochemistry-lecture.md` | In-depth lecture notes with reaction mechanisms and pathway logic |
| `WEAK-TOPICS.md` | Personally flagged weak areas requiring extra revision attention |

## High-Yield Areas

- Vitamins: deficiency diseases, daily requirements, fat-soluble vs water-soluble specifics — asked every year
- Enzyme kinetics: Km, Vmax, types of inhibition, allosteric regulation, and key regulatory enzymes
- Inborn errors of metabolism: phenylketonuria, alkaptonuria, galactosaemia, glycogen storage diseases, and lysosomal disorders
- Metabolic pathways: TCA cycle intermediates, urea cycle enzymes, glycolysis regulation, and fatty acid oxidation
- Molecular biology: PCR techniques, Southern/Northern/Western blot, restriction enzymes, and gene expression mechanisms
