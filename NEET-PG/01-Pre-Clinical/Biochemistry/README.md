# Biochemistry — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 10–12 questions (~5–6%)
**Tier:** 2

## Files in This Folder
- `biochemistry-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 2–3)
- `biochemistry-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Vitamins — deficiency diseases, functions, fat-soluble vs water-soluble specifics (asked every year)
2. Enzyme kinetics and regulation — Km, Vmax, inhibition types, key regulatory enzymes
3. Inborn errors of metabolism — phenylketonuria, alkaptonuria, galactosemia, glycogen storage diseases

## Study Tips
- Memorise the urea cycle and TCA cycle intermediates with their enzymes — these are direct Q sources.
- Molecular biology (PCR, Southern blot, restriction enzymes) is increasingly tested; do not skip it.
- Read Biochemistry before Pathology: understanding metabolic pathways makes pathology mechanisms logical rather than rote.
