# Physiology — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 12–14 questions (~6–7%)
**Tier:** 2

## Files in This Folder
- `physiology-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 2)
- `physiology-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Cardiovascular physiology — cardiac cycle, pressure-volume loops, Starling's law, ECG interpretation
2. Renal physiology — GFR, tubular handling, concentration mechanisms, acid-base regulation
3. Endocrine physiology — feedback mechanisms, hormone actions, pituitary-target organ axes

## Study Tips
- Derive, do not memorise: most renal and CVS Qs can be answered by applying core principles.
- Learn normal values (FVC, FEV1, dead space, GFR, RPF) — numerical Qs are easy marks.
- Respiratory physiology (V/Q ratio, shunts, hypoxia types) is cross-tested in Medicine and Anesthesia.
