# Pediatrics — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 12–13 questions (~6%)
**Tier:** 2

## Files in This Folder
- `pediatrics-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 6)
- `pediatrics-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Immunization schedule — UIP vaccines, doses, ages, cold chain, contraindications (update annually from MoHFW)
2. Developmental milestones — gross motor, fine motor, language, social milestones at key ages (4 weeks to 5 years)
3. Neonatology — RDS (surfactant, management), neonatal jaundice (phototherapy thresholds), birth asphyxia, APGAR scoring

## Study Tips
- The immunization schedule changes periodically — always use the current NMC/MoHFW schedule, not older textbook editions.
- Developmental milestone Qs have fixed numerical answers — memorise the milestone table as a grid (age vs domain).
- Fluid requirements, weight-based drug dosing, and nutritional requirements in children are calculation-based Qs that are easy marks.
