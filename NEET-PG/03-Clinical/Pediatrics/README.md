# Pediatrics

Pediatrics covers the growth, development, nutrition, immunization, and clinical management of diseases from the neonatal period through adolescence — contributing ~12 questions per NEET PG paper.

## Files in This Folder

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `pediatrics-notes.md` | Condensed exam-focused notes covering neonatology, development, nutrition, and paediatric diseases |
| `pediatrics-lecture.md` | In-depth lecture notes with milestone tables, immunization schedules, and clinical algorithms |
| `WEAK-TOPICS.md` | Personally flagged weak areas requiring extra revision attention |

## High-Yield Areas

- Immunization schedule: UIP vaccines, doses, ages, route, contraindications, cold chain management, and new vaccine additions
- Developmental milestones: gross motor, fine motor, language, and social milestones from 4 weeks to 5 years
- Neonatology: respiratory distress syndrome (surfactant therapy), neonatal jaundice (phototherapy thresholds, exchange transfusion criteria), APGAR scoring
- Nutritional disorders: PEM (marasmus vs kwashiorkor clinical distinction), vitamin D deficiency rickets, iron deficiency anaemia in children
- Paediatric emergencies: fluid calculations, oral rehydration therapy, management of febrile seizures, and meningitis in children
