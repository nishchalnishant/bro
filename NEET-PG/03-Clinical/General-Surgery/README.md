# General Surgery — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 15–16 questions (~7.5%)
**Tier:** 2

## Files in This Folder
- `surgery-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 5)
- `surgery-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Hepatobiliary surgery — obstructive jaundice (causes, Courvoisier's law, investigations), CBD stones, carcinoma pancreas
2. GIT surgery — intestinal obstruction (types, X-ray findings), colorectal carcinoma, appendicitis management
3. Hernias — inguinal (direct vs indirect), femoral, classification, complications, operative details

## Study Tips
- Surgery Qs often present with X-ray or imaging findings — learn characteristic radiological signs (Rigler's, Step-ladder, Bird-beak).
- Surgical anatomy (relations of structures, blood supply, lymphatic drainage) is directly tested — integrate with Anatomy.
- Fluids and electrolytes + burns management are reliable scoring topics that require formula-based answers.
