# General Medicine — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 22–24 questions (~12%)
**Tier:** 1

## Files in This Folder
- `medicine-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 4–5)
- `medicine-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Cardiology — IHD (STEMI criteria, management), heart failure (Framingham criteria, treatment), valvular diseases, arrhythmias (ECG interpretation)
2. Endocrinology — diabetes mellitus (diagnostic criteria, complications, insulin regimens), thyroid disorders (TFT interpretation), Cushing's, Addison's
3. Nephrology — CKD staging, nephrotic vs nephritic syndrome, acid-base disorders, renal tubular acidosis

## Study Tips
- Medicine questions are clinical case-based — practice reading long stems quickly and identifying the pivotal finding.
- Diagnostic criteria (ADA for DM, modified Duke for infective endocarditis, ACR for SLE, Framingham for HF) are direct, scorable points.
- ECG interpretation is asked every year — learn the systematic approach and common patterns (STEMI, LBBB, WPW, complete heart block).
