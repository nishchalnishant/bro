### Top 10 High-Yield Topics
1. Diabetes mellitus — diagnosis, targets, complications
2. Hypertension — JNC/ACC-AHA criteria, treatment algorithm
3. Thyroid disorders — diagnosis, investigations, management
4. Chest X-ray interpretation — common patterns
5. ECG interpretation — LBBB, RBBB, MI changes, arrhythmias
6. Rheumatoid arthritis — criteria, DMARDs
7. Nephrotic and nephritic syndromes
8. Meningitis — CSF interpretation
9. Fever with rash — differential diagnosis
10. Autoimmune conditions — ANA patterns, specific antibodies

> **Strategy:** Medicine has the highest question count in NEET PG. Topics 1–5 (DM, HTN, Thyroid, CXR, ECG) appear every single year — master these first.
