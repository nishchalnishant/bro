# Orthopedics — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 7 questions (~3.5%)
**Tier:** 3

## Files in This Folder
- `orthopedics-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 8)
- `orthopedics-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Fractures — Colles' fracture, femoral neck fractures (Garden classification), complications (AVN, compartment syndrome, fat embolism)
2. Bone tumors — osteosarcoma vs Ewing's sarcoma (age, X-ray pattern, histology, treatment)
3. Infections — acute osteomyelitis (organism, site, age group), septic arthritis management

## Study Tips
- X-ray findings are central to Orthopedics Qs — learn characteristic patterns (Codman's triangle, onion-peel, sunray spicules, Looser's zones).
- Compartment syndrome features (6 Ps) and emergency fasciotomy indication are always exam-ready.
- Eponymous tests (Thomas test, McMurray's, Lachman, anterior drawer) are direct Q sources — table them with the joint and what they detect.
