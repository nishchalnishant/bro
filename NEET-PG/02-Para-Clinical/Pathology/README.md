# Pathology — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 17–19 questions (~9%)
**Tier:** 1

## Files in This Folder
- `pathology-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 3)
- `pathology-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Hematology — anemias (classification, peripheral smear findings, indices), leukemias, lymphomas
2. Neoplasia — tumor markers, carcinogenesis, oncogenes, grading vs staging
3. Cell injury and inflammation — types of necrosis, granuloma-forming conditions, healing and repair

## Study Tips
- Learn pathology with images: peripheral blood smears, histopathology slides, and gross specimens are commonly described in question stems.
- Tumor markers are a guaranteed question source — maintain a table (CEA, AFP, CA-125, PSA, etc.).
- Granulomatous conditions (TB, sarcoidosis, Crohn's, leprosy, foreign body) need clear differential features.
