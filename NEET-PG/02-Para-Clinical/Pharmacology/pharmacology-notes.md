**Trend:** Stable — consistently 16–17 questions every year.

### Top 10 High-Yield Topics
1. Beta-blocker pharmacology — cardioselective, ISA, uses, contraindications
2. Antibiotics — mechanisms, spectrum, side effects
3. Anticoagulants and antiplatelets
4. Antiepileptics — mechanisms and teratogenicity
5. Antihypertensives — classes, MOA, compelling indications
6. Chemotherapy agents — mechanisms and toxicities
7. Opioid pharmacology — receptors, antagonists, uses
8. Organophosphate poisoning and management
9. Drug interactions (warfarin, MAOIs, serotonin syndrome)
10. Newer drugs — SGLT2i, GLP-1 agonists, DOACs, biologics

> **Strategy:** Drug side effects and mechanisms dominate. The side effects master table (`pharma-side-effects.md`) alone is worth 4–5 marks per paper.
