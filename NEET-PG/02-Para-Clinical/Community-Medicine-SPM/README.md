# Community Medicine (SPM)

Community Medicine (Social and Preventive Medicine) covers epidemiology, biostatistics, national health programs, nutrition, environmental health, and health administration — contributing ~16 questions per NEET PG paper with a rising trend.

## Files in This Folder

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `spm-notes.md` | Condensed exam-focused notes covering epidemiology, biostatistics, and national programs |
| `spm-lecture.md` | In-depth lecture notes with study design flowcharts and program details |
| `WEAK-TOPICS.md` | Personally flagged weak areas requiring extra revision attention |

## High-Yield Areas

- Epidemiology and biostatistics: study designs, sensitivity/specificity/PPV/NPV, relative risk, odds ratio, confidence intervals, and p-values
- National health programs: NHM, NTEP (RNTCP), NVBDCP, UIP — targets, beneficiaries, drug regimens, and coverage goals
- Nutrition: PEM (marasmus vs kwashiorkor differences), vitamin deficiency diseases, RDA values, ICDS program, and POSHAN Abhiyan
- Immunization: UIP schedule, cold chain management, vaccine contraindications, and new vaccine additions
- Health indicators and demography: IMR, MMR, TFR, CDR, fertility rates, and demographic transition model
