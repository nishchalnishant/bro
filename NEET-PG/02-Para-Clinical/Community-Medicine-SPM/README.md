# Community Medicine (SPM) — NEET PG
**NEET PG Weight:** 15–17 questions (~8%)
**Tier:** 1

## Files in This Folder
- `spm-lecture.md` — First-principles lecture notes for deep understanding (read in Month 6)
- `spm-notes.md` — Condensed exam-focused notes for revision (use from Month 9 onwards)

## Top NEET PG Topics
1. Epidemiology and biostatistics — study designs, sensitivity/specificity/PPV/NPV, relative risk, odds ratio, p-value, confidence intervals
2. National health programs — NHM, RNTCP/NTEP, NVBDCP, UIP — targets, beneficiaries, schedules (update annually)
3. Nutrition — PEM (marasmus vs kwashiorkor), vitamin deficiencies, recommended daily allowances, ICDS

## Study Tips
- SPM is highly factual and static (except national program statistics) — consistent revision pays off massively.
- Biostatistics Qs have formulaic answers; practice calculation-based Qs to convert these into automatic scoring.
- Update national program targets and immunization schedules from the MoHFW website every year — these numbers change and old books become incorrect.
