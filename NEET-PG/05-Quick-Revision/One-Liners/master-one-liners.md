## SPM / COMMUNITY MEDICINE (40 One-Liners)

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO definition of health | **"Complete physical, mental, and social well-being**, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity" |
| Formula for sensitivity | Sensitivity = **TP / (TP + FN)** × 100 |
| Formula for specificity | Specificity = **TN / (TN + FP)** × 100 |
| Formula for PPV | PPV = **TP / (TP + FP)** × 100 |
| Formula for NPV | NPV = **TN / (TN + FN)** × 100 |
| Sensitivity increases means | Fewer **false negatives**; better at ruling out disease (SnOut) |
| Screening test ideal combination | **High sensitivity** (for screening) + high specificity (for confirmation) |
