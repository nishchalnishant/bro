# Golden Topics Master List

Revise these repeatedly throughout the year.

## Pre-Clinical

- Brachial plexus, cranial nerves, embryology derivatives
- Physiology graphs, CVS formulas, renal acid-base
- Vitamins, inborn errors, rate-limiting enzymes

## Para-Clinical

- Inflammation, necrosis, tumor markers, leukemia and lymphoma
- ANS drugs, antibiotics, chemotherapy, antidotes
- Immunology, virology, vaccines, stains, culture media
- Epidemiology formulas, screening tests, health programs

## Clinical

- Cataract, glaucoma, uveitis, fundus images
- Ear diseases, vertigo, larynx, head and neck spaces
- ECG, shock, poisoning, acid-base, electrolyte disorders
- Trauma, thyroid, breast, acute abdomen, hernia
- Obstetric emergencies, CTG, contraception, infertility
- Neonatology, milestones, immunization
- Fractures, nerve injuries, bone tumors
- Leprosy, STDs, dermatological images
- Radiology signs and common image diagnosis
- Airway, spinal anesthesia, local anesthetics, CPR
