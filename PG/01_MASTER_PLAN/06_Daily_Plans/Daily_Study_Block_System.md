# Daily Study Block System

Use a simple repeatable model.

## Full-Time Preparation Day

- Block 1: major subject learning
- Block 2: major subject MCQs
- Block 3: minor subject learning or revision
- Block 4: PYQs or error notebook
- Block 5: rapid revision of old topics

## Internship Day

- Block 1: rapid revision
- Block 2: 25 to 50 MCQs
- Block 3: one focused topic

## Minimum Daily Non-Negotiables

- Revise something old
- Solve questions
- Log at least one mistake
- Update next-day plan before sleeping
