## Golden Topics To Master Early

These are disproportionately high-yield and should be revised repeatedly:

- Anatomy: nerves, blood supply, embryology derivatives, foramina
- Physiology: graphs, CVS, renal, endocrine
- Biochemistry: enzymes, pathways, vitamins, genetics
- Pathology: inflammation, neoplasia, hematology, images
- Pharmacology: ANS, antibiotics, CNS, chemotherapy, antidotes
- Microbiology: immunology, bacteriology, virology, lab tests, vaccines
- PSM: epidemiology, biostatistics, vaccines, health programs
- Medicine: ECG, ABG, emergencies, infectious disease, endocrinology
- Surgery: trauma, thyroid, breast, GIT, instruments
- OBG: obstetric emergencies, CTG, contraception, gynec oncology
- Pediatrics: neonatology, nutrition, immunization, milestones
- Ophthal and ENT: images, instruments, one-liners

## Minimum Output Per Subject

For every subject, complete all of the following:

- One concise note set
- One revision document
- PYQs solved
- QBank done at least once
- Mistakes logged
- High-yield image and table review
