# Obstetrics And Gynecology MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Labor, PPH, preeclampsia, and ectopic pregnancy.
- CTG, contraception, infertility, and AUB.
- Gynecologic malignancies and staging.

## Practice Method
- Solve obstetrics and gynecology separately first.
- Re-solve emergency obstetric MCQs weekly.
- Maintain one list for staging and contraindications.

## Accuracy Targets
- First pass: 60%+
- Second pass: 76%+
- Final phase: 86%+
