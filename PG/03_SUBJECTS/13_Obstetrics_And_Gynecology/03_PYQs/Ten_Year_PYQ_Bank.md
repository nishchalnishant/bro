# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: OBG (Obstetrics & Gynecology) (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on high-yield labor management, contraception choice, and updated screening protocols.

## 1. Obstetrics (Pregnancy & Labor)

### [Clinical Vignette] - Preeclampsia
**Question**: A pregnant woman at 34 weeks gestation presents with BP 160/110 mmHg and proteinuria. What is the drug of choice for seizure prophylaxis?
- **Key Point**: **Magnesium Sulfate (MgSO4)**. Protocol: Pritchard/Zuspan.
- **Year Repeated**: 2014, 2018, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Earliest sign of Eclampsia**: Facial twitching / Aura.
- **DOC for PPH (Post-partum Hemorrhage)**: Oxytocin (Initially), then Carboprost/Misoprostol.
- **Cardinal Movements of Labor**: Engagement -> Descent -> Flexion -> Internal Rotation -> Extension -> External Rotation -> Expulsion. (Mnemonic: Every Darn Fool In Egypt Eats Raw Eggs).

## 2. Gynecology (Oncology & Disorders)

### [Clinical Vignette] - Cervical Cancer Screening (Update)
**Question**: A 30-year-old female comes for cervical cancer screening. What is the current FIGO/WHO recommendation for screening?
- **Key Point**: **HPV DNA testing** (Primary screening). [Note: PAP smear used if HPV DNA not available].
- **Year Repeated**: 2021, 2023.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Most common type of Ovarian Cancer**: Serous Cystadenocarcinoma.
- **Meigs Syndrome**: Ovarian fibroma + Ascites + Right-sided Hydrothorax.
- **Chocolate Cyst of Ovary**: Endometriosis.
- **Snowstorm Appearance on USG**: Hydatidiform Mole.

## 3. Contraception & Hormones

### [Clinical Vignette] - Contraception Choice
**Question**: A lactating mother (4 months post-partum) wants a reliable oral contraceptive. Which option is preferred?
- **Key Point**: **Progesterone Only Pill (POP)** or Mini-pill. (Combined pills decrease milk production).
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Pearl Index**: Measure of contraceptive failure (Units: failures per 100 woman-years).
- **Ideal time for IUD insertion**: During menstruation or within 10 days of LMP.
- **Contraceptive of choice for Newly Married**: OCPs.

## 4. Reproductive Medicine

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Mayer-Rokitansky-Küster-Hauser (MRKH) Syndrome**: Primary Amenorrhea with normal 46,XX karyotype; Aplasia of Uterus/Vagina.
- **Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS)**: High LH/FSH ratio. Rottherdam Criteria used for diagnosis.
- **Asherman Syndrome**: Intrauterine adhesions (Secondary Amenorrhea).
