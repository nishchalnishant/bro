# Obstetrics And Gynecology High-Yield Notes

## Obstetrics Basics
- Normal pregnancy physiology, antenatal care, fetal circulation, and placenta.
- Pregnancy diagnosis, dating, and expected physiological changes.

## Labor And Obstetric Emergencies
- Stages of labor, partograph, induction, PPH, obstructed labor, and shoulder dystocia.
- Ectopic pregnancy, abortion, APH, PPH, and hypertensive disorders are core topics.

## High-Risk Pregnancy
- GDM, anemia, Rh isoimmunization, multiple pregnancy, IUGR, and fetal surveillance.
- CTG interpretation and Doppler basics are high yield.

## Gynecology
- Menstrual disorders, amenorrhea, infertility, PCOS, fibroid, adenomyosis, and endometriosis.
- PID, prolapse, contraception, and menopause should be revised repeatedly.

## Gynec Oncology And Instruments
- Cervix, endometrium, ovary, gestational trophoblastic disease, and FIGO staging basics.
- Speculum, forceps, IUCD, and operative obstetric instruments are frequently tested.
