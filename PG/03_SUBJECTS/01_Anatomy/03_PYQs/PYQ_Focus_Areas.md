# Anatomy PYQ Focus Areas

## Repeated PYQ Themes
- Nerve injury after fracture or dislocation.
- Brachial plexus root value and lesion pattern.
- Direct vs indirect inguinal hernia.
- Cranial nerve palsy and cavernous sinus.
- Branchial arch derivatives.
- Histology slide identification.
- Internal capsule blood supply.
- Femoral triangle and cubital fossa contents.
- Bronchopulmonary segments and mediastinum.
- Pelvic supports and pudendal nerve.

## PYQ Review Method
- Group PYQs by these buckets:
  upper limb, lower limb, thorax, abdomen, head-neck, neuroanatomy, embryology, histology.
- Track not only the answer, but the exam’s favorite distractor.
- Convert repeated PYQs into one-liners and place them in revision sheet.

## Most Repeated Facts
- Surgical neck of humerus -> axillary nerve.
- Neck of fibula -> common peroneal nerve.
- Corneal reflex -> `V1` afferent, `VII` efferent.
- Superior orbital fissure -> `III`, `IV`, `V1`, `VI`.
- Third pouch -> inferior parathyroid and thymus.
- Hassall corpuscles -> thymus.
