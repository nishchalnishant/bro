# Anatomy One-Liners For Final Revision

- Erb palsy is `C5-C6`.
- Klumpke palsy is `C8-T1`.
- Long thoracic nerve supplies serratus anterior.
- Quadrangular space contains axillary nerve.
- Neck of fibula injury causes foot drop.
- Right main bronchus is wider, shorter, more vertical.
- Coronary artery dominance depends on posterior interventricular branch.
- Indirect hernia lies lateral to inferior epigastric vessels.
- Pudendal nerve is `S2-S4`.
- Cavernous sinus contains ICA and abducens within sinus.
- Third nerve palsy causes down-and-out eye.
- Corneal reflex uses `V1` and `VII`.
- Third pouch forms thymus and inferior parathyroid.
- Hassall corpuscles identify thymus.
- Brunner glands identify duodenum.
- Peyer patches identify ileum.
