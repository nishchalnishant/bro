# Essential Anatomy Tables

These master tables are designed for quick comparison and cross-checking of related facts.

## 1. Urogenital Derivatives
| Embryonic Structure | Male Derivative | Female Derivative |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Paramesonephric (Mullerian)** | Appendix Testis | **Uterus, Tubes, Upper Vagina** |
| **Mesonephric (Wolffian)** | **Epididymis, Vas, Seminal Vesicle** | Appendix Vesiculosa |
| **Urogenital Sinus** | Bladder, Prostate, Urethra | Bladder, Lower Vagina, Urethra |
| **Gubernaculum** | Gubernaculum Testis | **Round Ligament**, Ligament of Ovary |

## 2. Inguinal Hernia Master Comparison
| Feature | Indirect Hernia | Direct Hernia |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Etiology** | Patent processus vaginalis | Weakness of abdominal wall |
| **Relation to Vessels** | **Lateral** to Inf. Epigastric | **Medial** to Inf. Epigastric |
| **Course** | Through inguinal canal | Through Hesselbach triad |
| **Touch Test** | Hits the **Tip** of the finger | Hits the **Side** of the finger |

## 3. Brachial Plexus Terminal Branches
| Nerve | Root Value | Muscle Supply (High Yield) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Axillary** | C5-C6 | Deltoid, Teres minor |
| **Radial** | C5-T1 | Triceps, Extensors of Forearm |
| **Median** | C5-T1 | Flexors of Forearm (mostly), Thenar |
| **Ulnar** | C8-T1 | Intrinsic muscles of Hand (mostly) |
| **Musculocutaneous** | C5-C7 | Biceps, Coracobrachialis |

## 4. Pelvic Diaphragm Components
- **Puborectalis**: U-shaped sling around the anorectal junction. Essential for fecal continence.
- **Pubococcygeus**: Main part of levator ani.
- **Iliococcygeus**: Posterior part of levator ani.
- **Coccygeus**: Not part of levator ani but part of the pelvic diaphragm.

## 5. Branchial Arch Skeletal Derivatives
| Arch | Skeletal Element |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1st** | Mandible, Malleus, Incus |
| **2nd** | Stapes, Styloid process, Lesser horn of hyoid |
| **3rd** | Greater horn of hyoid |
| **4th/6th** | Laryngeal cartilages (Thyroid, Cricoid, etc.) |

## 6. Major Foramina Levels
- **Vena Caval (T8)**: IVC, Right Phrenic.
- **Oesophageal (T10)**: Oesophagus, Vagus.
- **Aortic (T12)**: Aorta, Thoracic Duct, Azygos vein.

## 7. Fracture-Nerve Pairs
- Surgical neck humerus -> axillary nerve.
- Midshaft humerus -> radial nerve.
- Medial epicondyle -> ulnar nerve.
- Supracondylar humerus -> median nerve/brachial artery.
- Neck of fibula -> common peroneal nerve.

## Important Spaces
- Quadrangular:
  axillary nerve, posterior circumflex humeral artery.
- Triangular:
  circumflex scapular artery.
- Triangular interval:
  radial nerve, profunda brachii artery.

## Reflexes
- Corneal:
  `V1`, `VII`.
- Gag:
  `IX`, `X`.
- Pupillary light:
  `II`, `III`.
- Jaw jerk:
  trigeminal.

## Histology Differentiators
- Spleen:
  white pulp with central arteriole.
- Lymph node:
  cortex follicles and medullary sinuses.
- Thymus:
  lobules and Hassall corpuscles.
- Duodenum:
  Brunner glands.
- Ileum:
  Peyer patches.
- Colon:
  no villi, many goblet cells.
