## 2. Hernia Comparison (High Yield)
| Feature | Indirect Hernia | Direct Hernia |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Origin** | Thru Internal Ring | Thru Hesselbach Triangle |
| **Relation to Vessels** | **Lateral** to Inf. Epigastric | **Medial** to Inf. Epigastric |
| **Coverings** | Internal Spermatic Fascia | Only External Fascia |
| **Internal Ring Test** | Controlled | Not Controlled |

## 3. High-Yield Mnemonics
- **Motel Pass** (Neural Crest): Melanocytes, Odontoblasts, Tracheal cartilage, Enterochromaffin, Laryngeal cartilage, Parafollicular cells, Adrenal Medulla, Schwann cells, Spinal/Symp ganglia.
- **MALT** (Inguinal Canal): Muscles (2), Aponeurosis (1), Ligaments (1), Tendon (1).
- **MBBR** (Cubital Fossa): Median N, Brachial A, Biceps Tendon, Radial N.
- **NAVY** (Femoral Triangle): Nerve, Artery, Vein, Y-fronts (Lymphatics).
- **LR6 SO4** (Eye Muscles): Lateral Rectus (VI), Superior Oblique (IV), Rest (III).
