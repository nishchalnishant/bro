# Essential Anatomy Tables

## Fracture-Nerve Pairs
- Surgical neck humerus -> axillary nerve.
- Midshaft humerus -> radial nerve.
- Medial epicondyle -> ulnar nerve.
- Supracondylar humerus -> median nerve/brachial artery.
- Neck of fibula -> common peroneal nerve.

## Important Spaces
- Quadrangular:
  axillary nerve, posterior circumflex humeral artery.
- Triangular:
  circumflex scapular artery.
- Triangular interval:
  radial nerve, profunda brachii artery.

## Reflexes
- Corneal:
  `V1`, `VII`.
- Gag:
  `IX`, `X`.
- Pupillary light:
  `II`, `III`.
- Jaw jerk:
  trigeminal.

## Histology Differentiators
- Spleen:
  white pulp with central arteriole.
- Lymph node:
  cortex follicles and medullary sinuses.
- Thymus:
  lobules and Hassall corpuscles.
- Duodenum:
  Brunner glands.
- Ileum:
  Peyer patches.
- Colon:
  no villi, many goblet cells.
