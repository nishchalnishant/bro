# Anatomy Images And Tables Checklist

## Osteology
- Humerus, radius, ulna.
- Scapula and clavicle.
- Hip bone, femur, tibia, fibula.
- Skull base foramina.
- Vertebrae: cervical, thoracic, lumbar, atlas, axis.

## Histology Slides
- Epithelium types.
- Hyaline, elastic, fibrocartilage.
- Compact bone.
- Lymph node, spleen, thymus.
- Thyroid, parathyroid, pituitary.
- Liver, kidney, stomach, duodenum, ileum, colon.

## Gross/Dissection Images
- Brachial plexus.
- Cubital fossa.
- Femoral triangle.
- Mediastinum.
- Inguinal canal.
- Brain sections and ventricles.

## Radiological Anatomy
- CT chest orientation.
- MRI brain midline sections.
- X-ray skull foramina/landmarks.
- Spine sections.

## Must-Make Tables
- Brachial plexus branches.
- Cranial nerves and foramina.
- Foramina and transmitted structures.
- Branchial arch derivatives.
- Germ layer derivatives.
- Fracture-nerve pairs.
- Histology differentiators.
