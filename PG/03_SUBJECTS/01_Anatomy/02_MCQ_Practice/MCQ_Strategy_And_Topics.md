# Anatomy MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Brachial plexus and nerve lesions.
- Cranial nerves and brainstem syndromes.
- Embryology derivatives and branchial arches.
- Foramina, blood supply, and radiological anatomy.

## Practice Method
- Solve region-wise first, then mixed anatomy MCQs.
- Mark every nerve-root or artery question you miss.
- Re-solve image-based anatomy weekly.

## Accuracy Targets
- First pass: 55%+
- Second pass: 70%+
- Final phase: 80%+
