# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: Pediatrics (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on neonatal resuscitation, developmental milestones, and high-yield genetic/metabolic conditions.

## 1. Neonatology (Core Repeater)

### [Clinical Vignette] - Neonatal Resuscitation (NRP Update)
**Question**: A newborn is delivered with poor respiratory effort. APGAR score is 3. What is the most important initial step in resuscitation if the heart rate is < 100?
- **Key Point**: **Positive Pressure Ventilation (PPV)**. (2014, 2018, 2021, 2024).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **APGAR Scores**: Appearance, Pulse, Grimace, Activity, Respiration.
- **Surfactant Deficiency**: Respiratory Distress Syndrome (RDS) in preterm infants.
- **Earliest sign of Kernicterus**: Poor sucking reflex / Moro's reflex loss.

## 2. Growth & Development

### [Clinical Vignette] - Milestones
**Question**: At what age does a child typically start walking without support and say 2-3 words with meaning?
- **Key Point**: **12 Months (1 Year)**.
- **Year Repeated**: 2015, 2019, 2023.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Social Smile**: 2 months.
- **Stranger Anxiety**: 7-9 months.
- **Pincer Grasp**: 10 months.
- **Tricycle riding**: 3 years.

## 3. Pediatric Nutrition & Infections

### [Clinical Vignette] - PEM
**Question**: A 2-year-old child presents with "flaky paint" dermatitis, edema, and moon facies. Diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Kwashiorkor** (Protein deficiency).
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2022.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Marasmus**: Total calorie deficiency; "Old man facies"; No edema.
- **Koplik Spots**: Pathognomonic for **Measles** (Prodromal stage).
- **DOC for Kawasaki Disease**: IVIG + Aspirin.

## 4. Genetics & Congenital Disorders

### [Clinical Vignette] - Down Syndrome
**Question**: A child presents with flat facies, simian crease, and brushfield spots. What is the most common cause of intellectual disability in this condition?
- **Key Point**: **Trisomy 21**. (Most common cause: Meiotic Non-disjunction).
- **Year Repeated**: 2014, 2017, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Most common congenital heart disease**: Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD).
- **Most common cyanotic heart disease**: Tetralogy of Fallot (TOF).
- **Cystic Fibrosis mutation**: Delta F508 (CFTR gene).
- **Pyloric Stenosis**: "Olive-shaped mass"; String sign on barium.
