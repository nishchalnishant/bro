# Pediatrics MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Neonatology and immunization.
- Growth, development, and milestones.
- Diarrhea, dehydration, seizures, and CHD.

## Practice Method
- Solve age-based cases carefully.
- Repeat milestone and vaccine MCQs weekly.
- Maintain one page for neonatal emergencies.

## Accuracy Targets
- First pass: 60%+
- Second pass: 76%+
- Final phase: 86%+
