# Psychiatry High-Yield Notes

## Foundations
- Mental status examination, thought disorders, hallucinations, delusions, and insight levels.

## Major Disorders
- Schizophrenia, depression, bipolar disorder, anxiety disorders, OCD, and phobias.
- Suicide risk and red flag symptoms must be revised well.

## Substance Use And Organic Psychiatry
- Alcohol withdrawal, opioid use, sedatives, delirium, dementia, and personality disorders.

## Child And Sleep Disorders
- ADHD, autism, enuresis, intellectual disability, sleep disorders, and eating disorders.

## Treatment
- Antipsychotics, antidepressants, mood stabilizers, anxiolytics, ECT indications, and side effects.
