# Psychiatry MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Schizophrenia and mood disorders.
- Anxiety disorders and OCD.
- Substance use and withdrawal.
- Antipsychotic adverse effects and ECT.

## Practice Method
- Solve symptom-definition questions separately from drug questions.
- Keep one sheet for side effects and toxicities.
- Repeat child psychiatry and delirium-dementia comparisons.

## Accuracy Targets
- First pass: 60%+
- Second pass: 76%+
- Final phase: 86%+
