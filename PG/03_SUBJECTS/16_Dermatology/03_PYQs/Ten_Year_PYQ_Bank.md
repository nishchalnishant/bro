# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: Dermatology (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on pathognomonic clinical signs and "IBQ-heavy" diagnostic features of skin diseases.

## 1. Vesiculo-Bullous Disorders (Core Repeater)

### [Clinical Vignette] - Pemphigus Vulgaris
**Question**: A 45-year-old male presents with painful oral ulcers and flaccid blisters on the chest. **Nikolsky's Sign** is positive. Immunofluorescence shows a "Fish-net" pattern. Diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Pemphigus Vulgaris**. (Note: Target antigen is Desmoglein 3).
- **Year Repeated**: 2014, 2017, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Bullous Pemphigoid**: Tense blisters; Negative Nikolsky; Linear IgG on Basement Membrane Zone.
- **Dermatitis Herpetiformis**: Grouped vesicles on elbows/knees; Associated with **Celiac Disease**.
- **Acantholysis**: Separated keratinocytes (Seen in Pemphigus).

## 2. Psoriasis & Papulosquamous Disorders

### [Clinical Vignette] - Psoriasis
**Question**: A patient has silver-white scaly plaques on the extensor surfaces. Removal of a scale reveals pinpoint bleeding. What is the name of this clinic sign?
- **Key Point**: **Auspitz's Sign**.
- **Year Repeated**: 2015, 2019, 2023.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Koebner Phenomenon**: New lesions appear at the site of trauma (Psoriasis, Lichen Planus, Vitiligo).
- **Lichen Planus**: 5 Ps (Planar, Polygonal, Purple, Pruritic, Papules). **Wickham Striae** present.
- **Pityriasis Rosea**: "Herald Patch" followed by "Christmas Tree" distribution.

## 3. Infections & Infestations (IBQ Core)

### [Clinical Vignette] - Leprosy
**Question**: A patient presents with a hypo-pigmented, anesthetic patch on the forearm and a thickened peripheral nerve. Diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Leprosy (Hansen's Disease)**. (Note: Lepromatous = leonine facies, many bacilli; Tuberculoid = few bacilli, few lesions).
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Scabies**: Burrows in web spaces; Nocturnal pruritus.
- **Tinea Versicolor**: "Spaghetti and meatballs" appearance on KOH.
- **Molluscum Contagiosum**: Umbilicated papules; Henderson-Patterson bodies.

## 4. STIs & Miscellaneous

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Syphilis Chancre**: Painless, hard base.
- **Donovanosis (Chancroid)**: Painful, soft base.
- **Pellagra Triad**: Dermatitis, Diarrhea, Dementia (Niacin B3 Vit deficiency).
- **Herald Patch**: Pityriasis Rosea.
- **Target Lesion**: Erythema Multiforme (e.g., after HSV infection).
