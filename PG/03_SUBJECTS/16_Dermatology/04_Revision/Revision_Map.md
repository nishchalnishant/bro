# Dermatology Revision Map

## First Revision
- Leprosy, psoriasis, fungal infections, STDs, and vesicobullous diseases.

## Second Revision
- Drug eruptions, connective tissue disease, pigmentary disorders, and tumors.

## Final Revision
- Image-based lesions, Nikolsky sign disorders, and one-line differentiators.

## Must Recall Lists
- Leprosy classification.
- STD causative agents.
- Bullous disorder antibodies.
- Drug rash clues.
