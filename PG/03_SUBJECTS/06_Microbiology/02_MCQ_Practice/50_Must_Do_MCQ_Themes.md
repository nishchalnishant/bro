# 50 Must-Do MCQ Themes for Microbiology

Microbiology is high-scoring. Master these core themes:

1. **Sterilization Indicators**: Matching indicators (e.g., *Geobacillus*) to Autoclave/Hot air oven.
2. **Gram Stain Procedure**: Step-wise identifying the bug based on stain properties.
3. **Culture Media Recognition**: Identifying selective media (LJ, TCBS, Thayer-Martin) from images.
4. **Bacterial Colony Features**: Swarming (Proteus), Medusa-head (Anthrax), Black colonies (Diphtheria).
5. **Protein A & M Protein**: Recognizing the specific virulence factors of Staph and Strep.
6. **Toxic Shock Syndrome Toxin (TSST-1)**: Clinical vignette of a patient using tampons.
7. **Gas Gangrene**: Case of a road traffic accident with crepitus (C. perfringens).
8. **Botulism vs Tetanus**: Differentiating flaccid vs spastic paralysis.
9. **Satellitism in H. influenzae**: Identifying the dependence on Staph aureus for factors X & V.
10. **Pseudomonas Fruity Odor**: Recognizing the blue-green pigment (Pyocyanin) in burn patients.
11. **Vibrio Cholerae TCBS Identification**: Yellow colonies due to sucrose fermentation.
12. **Enteric Fever (Typhoid)**: Sequential diagnosis (Blood -> Widal -> Stool) and Pea-soup diarrhea.
13. **Syphilis Diagnosis**: Differentiating Screening (VDRL/RPR) vs Confirmatory (FTA-ABS).
14. **Leptospirosis**: "Dinger's Ring" in culture and history of water exposure.
15. **Hepatitis Serology Interpretation**: Identifying Acute, Chronic, and Window periods.
16. **Hepatitis E in Pregnancy**: High mortality risk themes.
17. **HIV Genes & Markers**: Identifying p24 (screening) and Western Blot (confirmatory).
18. **Herpes Simplex Inclusions**: Cowdry Type A intranuclear bodies.
19. **CMV "Owl-Eye" Inclusions**: Recognizing the pathognomonic histology.
20. **Rabies Negri Bodies**: Identifying intracytoplasmic inclusions in brain tissue.
21. **Dengue Serology**: NS1 (early) vs IgM (recovery).
22. **Infectious Mononucleosis**: Heterophile antibody (Paul-Bunnell test).
23. **Mycology Branching Patterns**: Acute (Aspergillus) vs Obtuse (Mucor).
24. **Germ Tube Test**: Identifying *Candida albicans*.
25. **Cryptococcus India Ink**: Recognizing the thick polysaccharide capsule.
26. **Dimorphic Fungi Locations**: "Pilot wheel" (Paracoccidioides) vs "Cigar shaped" (Sporothrix).
27. **Protozoal Cysts**: Quadrinucleated cyst of *Entamoeba histolytica*.
28. **Malaria Gametocytes**: Banana-shaped (*P. falciparum*).
29. **Kala Azar (Leishmania)**: Identifying Amastigotes (LD bodies) in bone marrow.
30. **Parasite Eggs Identification**: Bile-stained (Ascaris) vs Non-bile stained (Hookworm).
31. **Enterobius Vermicularis diagnosis**: Morning NIH swab for D-shaped eggs.
32. **Neurocysticercosis**: Case of seizures with "Starry sky" (calcified cysts) on CT.
33. **Hydatid Cyst (Echinococcus)**: History of sheep/dog contact and Casoni test.
34. **Hypersensitivity Type I**: Clinical cases of Asthma/Anaphylaxis.
35. **Hypersensitivity Type II**: Hemolytic disease of the newborn.
36. **Hypersensitivity Type III**: Serum sickness and Arthus reaction.
37. **Hypersensitivity Type IV**: Contact dermatitis and Mantoux test.
38. **Immunoglobulin Properties**: Which crosses placenta? (IgG), Which is in secretions? (IgA).
39. **Complement Pathway**: Differentiating Classical vs Alternative triggers.
40. **HLA-B27**: Ankylosing spondylitis and Reiter's syndrome.
41. **Biosafety Levels (BSL)**: Where to handle Anthrax (BSL-3) vs Ebola (BSL-4).
42. **Biomedical Waste Segregation**: Yellow (Human waste) vs Red (Recyclable Plastic).
43. **Antimicrobial Stewardship**: Use of Vancomycin for MRSA.
44. **MRSA mecA gene**: Mechanism of methicillin resistance.
45. **Nagler Reaction**: Identifying lecithinase of *C. perfringens*.
46. **Elek's Test**: Detecting diphtheria toxin.
47. **Weil-Felix Test**: Differentiating Rickettsial types.
48. **Montenegro Skin Test**: For Leishmaniasis.
49. **Casoni's Test**: For Hydatid disease.
50. **Vaccination Schedule 2023**: When to give MR, PCV, and Rotavirus.
