# Mycology, Parasitology, And Applied Microbiology

## 1. Mycology: Fungal Infections

### Opportunistic Mycoses
- **Candida albicans**: Germ tube test (+). Pseudohyphae.
- **Aspergillus**: **Acute angle branching** (45°) septate hyphae. Fruiting bodies (Conidia).
- **Mucormycosis (Rhizopus)**: **Obtuse angle branching** (90°) non-septate hyphae. Seen in Diabetic Ketoacidosis.
- **Cryptococcus neoformans**: Polysaccharide **Capsule**. Diagnosis: **India Ink** (Halos). Bird-dropping association.

### Dimorphic Fungi (Mnemonic: "Body Heat Probably Changes Shape")
- **B**lastomyces, **H**istoplasma (Bird/Bat droppings), **P**aracoccidiodes (Pilot wheel), **C**occidioides, **S**porothrix (Cigar-shaped).

## 2. Parasitology: Diagnostic Morphology

### Master Egg Table (Very High Yield)
| Parasite | Egg Morphology | Special Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ascaris lumbricoides** | Round/Oval, **Bile-stained** | Mamillated coat |
| **Hookworm (Ancylostoma)** | Oval, **Non-bile stained** | Segmented (4-8 cells) |
| **Enterobius (Pinworm)** | **Planoconvex** (D-shaped) | NIH Swab for diagnosis |
| **Trichuris (Whipworm)** | **Barrel-shaped** | Bipolar mucus plugs |
| **Taenia solium** | Round, Brown coat | Radially striated |

### Protozoa & Life Cycles
- **Malaria**: 
    - **P. falciparum**: Banana-shaped gametocytes; Maurer's dots.
    - **P. vivax**: Schuffner's dots; Amoeboid trophozoite.
- **Leishmania**: Amastigote (LD body) in macrophages.
- **Entamoeba histolytica**: Anchovy-sauce pus in liver abscess. **Quadrinucleated cyst**.

## 3. Applied Microbiology & Infection Control

### Biomedical Waste Management (2016 Guidelines)
- **Yellow Bag**: Human anatomical waste, soiled waste (Blood/Pus), dressings.
- **Red Bag**: Tubings, catheters, gloves, vacutainers (Recyclable plastic).
- **White Translucent (Puncture proof)**: Sharps, needles.
- **Blue Box**: Glassware (broken), ampoules.

### Hospital-Acquired Infections (HAI)
- **MRSA**: Resists Methicillin (mecA gene). Treatment: **Vancomycin**.
- **VRE**: Resists Vancomycin (vanA gene). Treatment: **Linezolid**.
- **VAP (Ventilator Associated Pneumonia)**: Pseudomonas, Acinetobacter.
