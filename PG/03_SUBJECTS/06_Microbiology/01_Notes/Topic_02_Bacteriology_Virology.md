# Bacteriology And Virology

## Bacteriology
- Staphylococcus, Streptococcus, Enterobacteriaceae, Pseudomonas, Vibrio, Clostridia, Mycobacteria.
- Focus on morphology, toxins, tests, and diseases.

## Virology
- DNA and RNA virus classification, hepatitis viruses, HIV, herpes viruses, rabies, arboviruses.
- Inclusion bodies and vaccine associations are important.

## Must Learn
- Special culture media.
- Toxin-mediated diseases.
- Viral inclusion bodies.
- Hepatitis serology patterns.
