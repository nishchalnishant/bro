# Bacteriology And Virology

## 1. Bacteriology: Pathogenic Bacteria

### Gram-Positive Cocci
- **Staphylococcus aureus**: Catalase (+), Coagulase (+). Gold yellow colonies. **Protein A** (Antiphagocytic). Toxins: TSST-1, Exfoliative toxin (Scalded skin syndrome).
- **Streptococcus pyogenes (Group A)**: Large zone of beta-hemolysis. **M Protein** (Virulence). Causes RF, PSGN.
- **Streptococcus pneumoniae**: Diplococci; Capsule (+). Quellung reaction (+). **Optochin Sensitive**.

### Gram-Negative Bacilli
- **Pseudomonas aeruginosa**: Blue-green pigment (Pyocyanin). Fruity odor. Common in burns and Cystic Fibrosis.
- **Vibrio cholerae**: Darting motility. Selective media: **TCBS** (Yellow colonies). String test (+).
- **Escherichia coli**: Most common cause of UTI. ETEC (Traveler's diarrhea).

### Spore Formers & Toxins
- **Bacillus anthracis**: Medusa head colonies. Bamboo stick appearance. Spore (central).
- **Clostridium tetani**: Drumstick appearance (Terminal spores). Toxin: Tetanospasmin (Blocks GABA/Glycine).
- **Clostridium botulinum**: Blocks Acetylcholine release (Flaccid paralysis).

- **Hepatitis E**: High mortality in **pregnant women**.
- **Hepatitis D**: Needs HBsAg for replication (Superinfection vs Co-infection).

### HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus)
- **Genes**: *gag* (p24 - earliest marker), *pol* (Reverse transcriptase), *env* (gp120 - attachment, gp41 - fusion).
- **Diagnosis**: Screening (ELISA), Confirmatory (Western Blot - now p24/HIV-RNA preferred).
- **CDC Staging**: Based on CD4 count (<200 = AIDS).

### Viral Inclusion Bodies
- **Intranuclear**: 
    - **Cowdry Type A**: HSV, VZV.
    - **Cowdry Type B**: Polio, Adenovirus.
    - **Owl-eye**: CMV.
- **Intracytoplasmic**: 
    - **Negri Bodies**: Rabies.
    - **Guarnieri Bodies**: Variola (Smallpox).
    - **Henderson-Patterson**: Molluscum contagiosum.
- **Both**: Measles (Warthin-Finkeldey giant cells).
