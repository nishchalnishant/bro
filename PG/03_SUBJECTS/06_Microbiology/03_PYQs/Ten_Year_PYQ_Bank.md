# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: Microbiology (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on the high-yield "Visual Identification" (IBQs) and serology-based clinical vignettes.

## 1. General Microbiology & Immunology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Hypersensitivity
**Question**: A 25-year-old female develops a rash and wheezing within minutes of a bee sting. Which mediator is primarily responsible?
- **Key Point**: **IgE** (Type I Hypersensitivity). (2016, 2021).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Autoclave biological indicator**: *Geobacillus stearothermophilus*.
- **Hot Air Oven biological indicator**: *Bacillus atrophaeus*.
- **HLA-B27 associated disease**: Ankylosing spondylitis.
- **IgG property**: Only Ig to cross the **Placenta**.

## 2. Bacteriology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Vibrio Cholerae
**Question**: A patient presents with profuse rice-water stools. Culture on a selective medium shows **Yellow colonies**. Which medium was used?
- **Key Point**: **TCBS** (Thiosulfate Citrate Bile Salts Sucrose). (2015, 2019, 2024).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Medusa head colony**: *Bacillus anthracis*.
- **Protein A**: Virulence factor of *Staph aureus*.
- **Swarming motility**: *Proteus*.
- **Nagler reaction**: Identifies lecithinase of *Clostridium perfringens*.

## 3. Virology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Hepatitis B (Serology)
**Question**: A medical student gets a needle stick injury. Laboratory reports show **HBsAg (-)** and **Anti-HBs (+)**. What is the interpretation?
- **Key Point**: **Immune due to Vaccination**. (Repeated almost every year).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Negri Bodies**: Pathognomonic for **Rabies**.
- **Owl-eye inclusion**: Seen in **CMV**.
- **Smallest Virus**: Parvovirus.
- **Largest Virus**: Poxvirus.

## 4. Mycology & Parasitology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Mycology
**Question**: A diabetic patient presents with facial pain and a black eschar on the palate. Fungal culture shows **Broad, non-septate hyphae branching at 90°**. Diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Mucormycosis (Rhizopus)**. (2020, 2022 NEET PG).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **India Ink**: Used to identify **Cryptococcus** (Capsule).
- **Barrel-shaped egg**: *Trichuris trichura* (Whipworm).
- **D-shaped egg (Planoconvex)**: *Enterobius vermicularis* (Pinworm).
- **Banana-shaped gametocyte**: *Plasmodium falciparum*.
