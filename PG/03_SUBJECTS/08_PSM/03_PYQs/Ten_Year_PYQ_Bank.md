# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: PSM (Preventive & Social Medicine) (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on epidemiology, health indices, and the latest national health programs.

## 1. Epidemiology & Biostatistics

### [Clinical Vignette] - Study Design
**Question**: An investigator wants to find the **Association** between smoking and lung cancer. He identifies 100 cases of lung cancer and 100 healthy controls and looks back at their smoking history. What study design is this?
- **Key Point**: **Case-Control Study** (Retrospective). Odds Ratio is the measure of association.
- **Year Repeated**: 2014, 2018, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Incidence**: Number of **NEW** cases in a population.
- **Prevalence**: Number of **TOTAL** cases (New + Old).
- **p-value**: Probability of committing Type I error (Significant if <0.05).
- **Standard Deviation**: Measure of dispersion of data around the mean.

## 2. Health Indices & Demography

### [Clinical Vignette] - Neonatal Mortality
**Question**: A baby dies at the age of 20 days. How will this death be classified?
- **Key Point**: **Neonatal Mortality** (Death before 28 days).
- **Year Repeated**: 2017, 2023.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Maternal Mortality Ratio (MMR)**: Number of maternal deaths per **1 lakh (100,000)** live births.
- **Infant Mortality Rate (IMR)**: Number of deaths under 1 year per **1,000** live births.
- **Dependency Ratio**: Ratio of (0-14 + 65+) to the working population (15-64).

## 3. Communicable & Non-Communicable Diseases

### [Clinical Vignette] - NHP (Update)
**Question**: Under the National Tuberculosis Elimination Program (NTEP), what is the frequency of follow-up for a patient on the Intensive Phase?
- **Key Point**: Every month (End of 2nd month). [Note: DOTS is now replaced by Daily Fixed Dose Combination].
- **Year Repeated**: 2018, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Rule of Halves**: Applies to **Hypertension** (Management levels).
- **Iceberg Phenomenon**: Seen in most diseases; "Floating tip" = Diagnosed/Symptomatic; "Submerged" = Undiagnosed/Subclinical.
- **Hardness of Water**: Measured in mg/L or milliequivalents.

## 4. Environment & Nutrition

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Chlorination of Water**: Contact time of **30 minutes**. Free residual chlorine should be 0.5 mg/L.
- **Goitre**: Most common cause is Iodine deficiency.
- **Vitamin A Deficiency**: Bitot's spots (Earliest clinical sign).
- **Quetelet Index**: BMI (Weight/Height²).
