# Epidemiology And Biostatistics

## Epidemiology
- Incidence, prevalence, risk, odds ratio, attributable risk, bias, confounding, and causation.
- Outbreak investigation and surveillance are highly testable.

## Biostatistics
- Mean, median, mode, SD, standard error, p-value, confidence interval, and tests of significance.
- Screening test interpretation is one of the most repeated areas in PSM.

## Must Learn
- Study design comparison.
- Sensitivity/specificity formulas.
- Error types.
- Screening criteria.
