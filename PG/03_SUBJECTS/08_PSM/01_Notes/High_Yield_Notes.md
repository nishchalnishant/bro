# PSM High-Yield Notes

## Epidemiology And Biostatistics
- Incidence, prevalence, sensitivity, specificity, predictive values, and bias.
- Study designs, screening tests, standard deviation, p-value, and confidence interval.

## Communicable Diseases
- TB, malaria, dengue, rabies, HIV, leprosy, diarrheal diseases, and outbreak control.
- Modes of transmission, prevention, and surveillance systems.

## Non-Communicable Diseases
- Hypertension, diabetes, cancers, obesity, mental health, and occupational health.

## MCH And Nutrition
- Antenatal care, immunization, family planning, infant feeding, and nutrition programs.
- PEM, micronutrient deficiency, growth charts, and reproductive indicators.

## Health Programs And Environment
- National health programs, health planning, indicators, waste management, and water sanitation.
- Biomedical waste, cold chain, ventilation, and vector control are frequent.
