# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: Radiology (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on identifying Pathognomonic X-ray/CT/MRI signs and Radiation safety facts.

## 1. Thoracic & Cardiac Radiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Heart Shapes (IBQ)
**Question**: An X-ray chest shows a "Boot-shaped" heart with oligemic lung fields. What is the diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Tetralogy of Fallot (TOF)**. (2014, 2018, 2023).

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Egg-on-a-string appearance**: Transposition of Great Arteries (TGA).
- **Figure of 8 appearance**: Total Anomalous Pulmonary Venous Connection (TAPVC) - Supracardiac.
- **Air-bronchogram sign**: Seen in **Consolidation** (e.g., Pneumonia).
- **Sail Sign**: Normal thymus in young children.

## 2. Abdominal & GIT Radiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Bowel Obstruction (IBQ)
**Question**: An abdominal X-ray of an elderly male shows a "Coffee-bean" sign. Diagnosis?
- **Key Point**: **Sigmoid Volvulus**.
- **Year Repeated**: 2015, 2019, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Apple Core Sign**: Colorectal Carcinoma (Annular growth).
- **String Sign of Kantor**: Crohn's Disease.
- **Double Bubble Sign**: Duodenal Atresia.
- **Steeple Sign**: Croup (Subglottic narrowing) on AP view of neck.

## 3. Neuro & MSK Radiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Head Trauma (IBQ)
**Question**: A CT scan of a patient with head trauma shows a **"Biconvex / Lentiform"** hyperdense collection. Diagnosis and Vessel?
- **Key Point**: **Extradural Hemorrhage (EDH)**. Vessel: **Middle Meningeal Artery**.
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2021, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Crescentic / Concave collection**: Subdural Hemorrhage (SDH). Vessel: Bridging veins.
- **Star-shaped hemorrhage**: Subarachnoid Hemorrhage (SAH) in basal cisterns.
- **Sunray appearance**: Osteosarcoma.
- **Onion-skin appearance**: Ewing's Sarcoma.

## 4. Radiation Safety & Physics

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Most radiosensitive cell**: Lymphocytes (General); Spermatogonia (Gonads).
- **Investigation of Choice for Cholelithiasis**: Ultrasound (USG).
- **Investigation of Choice for Ureteric Calculus**: Non-contrast CT (NCCT KUB).
- **Hounsfield Unit (HU) of Water**: 0. (Air: -1000, Bone: +1000).
