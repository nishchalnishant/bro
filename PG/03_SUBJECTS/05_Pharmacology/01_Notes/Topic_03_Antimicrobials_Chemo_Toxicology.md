# Antimicrobials, Chemotherapy, And Toxicology

## 1. Antimicrobials

### Mechanism-Based Classification
- **Cell Wall Inhibitors**: Penicillins, Cephalosporins, Vancomycin.
- **Protein Synthesis Inhibitors**:
    - **30S**: Aminoglycosides (Ototoxic/Nephrotoxic), Tetracyclines.
    - **50S**: Macrolides (Erythromycin), Chloramphenicol (Gray baby syndrome).
- **DNA Gyrase Inhibitors**: Fluoroquinolones (Ciprofloxacin).
- **Antifolates**: Sulfonamides, Trimethoprim.

### Tuberculosis (NTEP Guidelines)
- **First-line Drugs**:
    - **Isoniazid (H)**: S/E: Peripheral neuropathy (Prevent with B6), Hepatotoxicity.
    - **Rifampin (R)**: MOA: Inhibits DNA-dependent RNA polymerase. S/E: Orange-colored urine/secretions.
    - **Pyrazinamide (Z)**: S/E: Hyperuricemia (Gout).
    - **Ethambutol (E)**: S/E: Optic neuritis (Red-green color blindness).
- **Regimen**: 2 months HRZE (Intensive) + 4 months HRE (Continuation).

### Malaria (NVBDCP 2023)
- **Drug of Choice for P. falciparum**: **Artesunate + Lumefantrine** (ACT).
- **Drug of Choice for P. vivax**: Chloroquine (3 days) + Primaquine (14 days - for hypnozoites/prevent relapse). **Check G6PD status before Primaquine**.

## 2. Cancer Chemotherapy

### MOA & Target Specificity
- **Alkylating Agents**: Cyclophosphamide (S/E: Hemorrhagic cystitis; prevent with **MESNA**).
- **Antimetabolites**: 
    - Methotrexate (S/E: Marrow suppression; rescue with **Leucovorin/Folinic acid**).
    - 5-Fluorouracil.
- **Microtubule Inhibitors**: Vincristine (S/E: Peripheral neuropathy), Paclitaxel.
- **Topoisomerase Inhibitors**: Etoposide, Irinotecan.
- **Targeted Therapy**: **Imatinib** (DOC for CML/GIST), Trastuzumab (HER2+ Breast Ca).
