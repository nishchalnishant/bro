## 2. Antidotes Master List (Recap)
- **Atropine**: Organophosphates / Mushroom poisoning.
- **Flumazenil**: Benzodiazepines.
- **Fomepizole**: Methanol / Ethylene Glycol.
- **Digibind**: Digoxin.
- **Glucagon**: Beta-blocker overdose.
- **N-Acetylcysteine**: Paracetamol (NAPQI neutralization).
- **Idarucizumab**: Dabigatran.
- **Andexanet alfa**: Rivaroxaban / Apixaban.
- **Leucovorin**: Methotrexate rescue.
- **Protamine Sulfate**: Heparin reversal.

## 3. Rapid-Recall One-Liners
1. **Zero-Order Drugs**: Phenytoin, Ethanol, Aspirin (High dose), Warfarin.
2. **Gray Baby Syndrome**: Chloramphenicol.
3. **Red Man Syndrome**: Vancomycin (Infusion rate related).
4. **Teratogenic ACE inhibitors**: Cause renal agenesis in fetus.
5. **Thalidomide**: Causes Phocomelia (seal-like limbs).
6. **Disulfiram-like Reaction**: Metronidazole, Cefotetan, Procarbazine.
7. **Ototoxic drugs**: Aminoglycosides, Loop diuretics, Cisplatin.
8. **Cinconism**: Quinidine / Quinine side effect.
9. **Xanthopsia (Yellow vision)**: Digoxin.
10. **Agranulocytosis**: Clozapine, Antithyroid drugs.
11. **Gingival Hyperplasia**: Phenytoin, Cyclosporine, Nifedipine.
12. **G6PD Trigger Drugs**: Primaquine, Sulfonamides, Nitrofurantoin.
13. **Photosensitivity**: Tetracyclines, Sulfonamides, Amiodarone.
14. **First dose syncope**: Prazosin.
15. **Hemorrhagic cystitis**: Cyclophosphamide (Prevent with Mesna).
16. **Pulmonary Fibrosis**: Busulfan, Bleomycin, Amiodarone.
17. **Gynecomastia**: Spironolactone, Cimetidine, Ketoconazole.
18. **Orange urine**: Rifampin.
19. **Pseudomembranous Colitis**: Caused by Clindamycin; Treated by Vancomycin/Fidaxomicin.
20. **Tendon Rupture**: Fluoroquinolones (Ciprofloxacin).
