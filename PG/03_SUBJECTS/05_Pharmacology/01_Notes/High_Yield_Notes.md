# Pharmacology High-Yield Notes

## General Pharmacology
- Pharmacokinetics, receptor pharmacology, adverse drug reactions, and drug interactions.
- First-pass metabolism, bioavailability, therapeutic index, and enzyme induction/inhibition.

## ANS And CNS Drugs
- Sympathomimetics, sympatholytics, cholinergic, anticholinergic, and NMJ blockers.
- Antiepileptics, antipsychotics, antidepressants, sedatives, opioids, and anesthetic basics.

## CVS, Renal, And Endocrine
- Antihypertensives, antianginals, antiarrhythmics, heart failure drugs, and diuretics.
- Thyroid, antidiabetic, steroids, contraceptives, and osteoporosis drugs.

## Antimicrobials
- Cell wall inhibitors, protein synthesis inhibitors, quinolones, sulfonamides, and TB drugs.
- Antifungals, antivirals, antimalarials, antihelminthics, and antileprosy therapy.

## Chemotherapy And Miscellaneous
- Anticancer classes, rescue agents, toxicities, immunosuppressants, and biologics.
- NSAIDs, antigout drugs, antihistamines, GI drugs, and respiratory drugs.

## Toxicology
- Common poisonings, antidotes, toxidromes, and emergency drug use.
