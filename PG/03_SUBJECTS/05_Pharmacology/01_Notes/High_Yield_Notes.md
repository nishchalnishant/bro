# Pharmacology High-Yield Notes

## What Matters Most
- ANS and CNS drug prototypes.
- Antibiotics and anti-TB drugs.
- Adverse effects and antidotes.
- CVS and endocrine drugs.
- Anticancer toxicities.

## How To Study
- Use drug prototype -> mechanism -> use -> adverse effect -> contraindication.
- Build drug of choice and antidote lists.

## Highest Yield Areas
- ANS.
- Antibiotics.
- Anti-TB.
- Antiepileptics.
- Antipsychotics.
- Antihypertensives.
- Insulin/OHAs.
- Poisoning.
