# Pharmacology PYQ Focus Areas

## Repeated PYQ Themes
- Mechanism-adverse effect pairs.
- CYP inducers and inhibitors.
- Antiarrhythmics and antihypertensives.
- Poison-antidote and anti-TB adverse effects.

## PYQ Workflow
- Tag each PYQ by mechanism, use, adverse effect, or antidote.
- Maintain separate repeat lists for autonomics and antimicrobials.
