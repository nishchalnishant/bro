# PYQ Focus Areas for Pharmacology

Based on an audit of NEET PG, INI-CET, and FMGE papers (2021-2024), prioritize these core focus clusters:

## 1. Guideline-Based Changes (Very High Yield)
- **Asthma/COPD**: Focus on the shift toward SMART therapy with **Formoterol-ICS** (GINA 2023).
- **Diabetes**: Strong emphasis on **SGLT2 inhibitors** and **GLP-1 agonists** (Mounjaro, Ozempic trends).
- **Hypertension**: Updates on DOC in elderly, pregnancy, and diabetic patients.

## 2. Monoclonal Antibodies (The "Mab" Surge)
- **Mechanism & Uses**: Frequent questions on **Trastuzumab** (HER2+), **Imatinib** (Bcl-Abl), **Rituximab** (CD20), and **Infliximab** (TNF-alpha).
- **New FDA Approvals**: Briefly review recent Alzheimer's drugs (Lecanemab) and Anti-obesity drugs (Tirzepatide).

## 3. Mechanisms of Action (Conceptual)
- **Antiepileptics**: Differentiating Na+ vs Ca+ vs GABA mechanisms.
- **Antimicrobials**: Classifying based on "Cell wall" vs "Protein synthesis" (30S/50S).
- **Digitalis**: Molecular mechanism of the Na+/K+ ATPase pump.

## 4. Adverse Drug Reactions (Factual)
- **The "High-Value" ADRs**: Amiodarone's multi-organ toxicity, Methotrexate marrow suppression, and Phenytoin's fetal syndrome.
- **Differentiating toxicities**: Comparing Ototoxicity vs Nephrotoxicity across drug classes.

## 5. Toxicology & Antidotes
- **Toxidrome recognition**: Identifying "Pinpoint pupil" (Opioid) vs "Dry as a bone" (Anticholinergic).
- **Newer Antidotes**: Adexanet alfa and Idarucizumab (NOAC reversals).

## 6. General Pharma Math
- **VD/Clearance/Loading Dose**: Practical calculations of dose adjustments.
- **Half-life**: Calculating time to steady state.
