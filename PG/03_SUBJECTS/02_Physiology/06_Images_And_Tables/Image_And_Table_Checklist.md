# Image And Table Checklist: Physiology

Mastering these 30 graphs will cover ~70% of the Physiology section in NEET PG.

## 1. Cardiovascular System (CVS)
1. **Wiggers Diagram**: Identifying the correlation between ECG, Heart Sounds, and Pressure changes.
2. **Pressure-Volume Loop**: Identifying valvular lesions (AS, AR, MS, MR).
3. **Frank-Starling Curve**: Effect of inotropes and heart failure.
4. **Cardiac Function vs Vascular Function Curve**: Identifying the Mean Systemic Filling Pressure (MSFP).
5. **JVP Waveform**: Cannon a-waves vs prominent x/y descents.

## 2. Respiratory System
1. **Oxygen-Hemoglobin Dissociation Curve**: Shift Right (CADET) vs Shift Left.
2. **Spirometry Graph**: Identifying TV, IRV, ERV, RV.
3. **Flow-Volume Loop**: Obstructive (Scooped out) vs Restrictive (Tall and Narrow).
4. **Compliance Curve**: Lung vs Chest wall (and combined).
5. **V/Q Ratio along the lung**: From Apex to Base.

## 3. Renal & Fluids
1. **Renal Clearance Curve**: Inulin (Linear) vs Glucose (Titration curve with Tmax).
2. **Countercurrent Multiplier**: Osmolality changes along the Loop of Henle.
3. **Darrow-Yannet Diagram**: Volume and Osmolality shifts in Dehydration, Infusion, and SIADH.
4. **Davenport Diagram**: Acid-Base compensation plots.

## 4. Neuro & Muscle
1. **Nerve Action Potential**: Depolarization vs Repolarization phases.
2. **Muscle Length-Tension Curve**: Active vs Passive tension.
3. **EEG Waveforms**: Alpha, Beta, Theta, Delta waves differentiation.
4. **Dark Adaptation Curve**: Rod vs Cone adaptation timings.
5. **Strength-Duration Curve**: Rheobase and Chronaxie.

## 5. Endocrine & Reproductive
1. **Menstrual Cycle Hormone Curve**: Correlation of FSH, LH, Estrogen, and Progesterone.
2. **Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT)**: Diabetic vs Impaired vs Normal curves.
3. **Thyroid Feedback Axis**: TSH vs Free T4 plots.
4. **Cortisol Diurnal Rhythm**: Peak (morning) vs Trough (midnight).

## 6. General
1. **Enzyme Kinetics (Michaelis-Menten)**: Km and Vmax changes in competitive vs non-competitive inhibition.
2. **Gastic Acid Secretion vs pH**: Effect of Vagotomy or PPIs.
3. **Growth Charts**: Identifying height/weight percentiles.
4. **Cardiac cycle graph.**
5. **ECG waves and intervals.**
6. **Hormone receptor-second messenger table.**
