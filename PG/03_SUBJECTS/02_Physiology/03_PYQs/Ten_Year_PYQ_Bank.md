# Ten-Year PYQ Bank: Physiology (2014-2024)

This bank focuses on core regulatory mechanisms (Kidney, Respiration, CVS) and high-yield receptor facts.

## 1. General & Nerve-Muscle Physiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Myasthenia Gravis
**Question**: A patient presents with muscle weakness that improves with rest but worsens towards the end of the day. What is the molecular target of the antibodies involved?
- **Key Point**: **Post-synaptic Nicotinic ACh Receptors** (NMJ).
- **Year Repeated**: 2015, 2019, 2023.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Resting Membrane Potential (RMP)**: Primarily determined by **K+ permeability**.
- **All-or-None Law**: Action potentials follow this; graded potentials do not.
- **Myelin sheath function**: Increases conduction velocity via **Saltatory Conduction**.

## 2. Cardiovascular & Respiratory Physiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Cardiac Cycle
**Question**: During which phase of the cardiac cycle is coronary blood flow to the **Left Ventricle** maximal?
- **Key Point**: **Early Diastole** (Isovolumetric relaxation).
- **Year Repeated**: 2014, 2017, 2021.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **Law of Laplace**: Pressure = 2T/r (Relevant to alveolar stability/surfactant).
- **Frank-Starling Law**: Stroke volume increases in response to an increase in end-diastolic volume.
- **Functional Residual Capacity (FRC)**: Volume of air in lungs after normal expiration.

## 3. Renal & Gastrointestinal Physiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Counter-Current Mechanism
**Question**: Which part of the nephron is primarily responsible for generating the medullary osmotic gradient?
- **Key Point**: **Loop of Henle** (Especially the ascending limb which is impermeable to water).
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2020, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2020 High Yield
- **GFR Marker (Gold Standard)**: **Inulin clearance**. (Clinical standard: Creatinine).
- **Gastrin Source**: G-cells of the gastric antrum.
- **B12 Absorption Site**: Terminal Ileum.

## 4. Endocrine & Neuro-Physiology

### [Clinical Vignette] - Thyroid Regulation
**Question**: A patient has high TSH and low T3/T4. What is the physiological classification of this state?
- **Key Point**: **Primary Hypothyroidism** (Failure of negative feedback at pituitary level).
- **Year Repeated**: 2016, 2019, 2024.

### [One-Liners] - 2014-2024 High Yield
- **Post-ovulatory hormone**: **Progesterone** (Secreted by Corpus Luteum).
- **Milk Ejection Hormone**: Oxytocin.
- **Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB)**: Formed by **Tight junctions** of endothelial cells and Astrocyte foot processes.
- **Sleep Stage of Dreams**: REM (Rapid Eye Movement) Sleep.
