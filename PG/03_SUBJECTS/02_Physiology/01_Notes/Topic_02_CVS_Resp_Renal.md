# CVS, Respiratory, And Renal Physiology

## Cardiovascular System
- Cardiac cycle, pressure-volume loops, heart sounds, ECG, cardiac output, and BP regulation.
- Baroreceptor reflex and venous return are repeatedly tested.

## Respiratory System
- Ventilation, perfusion, diffusion, dead space, compliance, and oxygen transport.
- Hypoxia types, oxygen dissociation curve, and lung volumes need active recall.

## Renal System
- GFR, tubular handling, concentration-dilution, countercurrent mechanism, and RAAS.
- Renal clearance and acid-base integration are must-know systems.

## Must Learn
- Pressure-volume loop interpretation.
- Oxygen dissociation shifts.
- Renal clearance formulas.
- Acid-base compensation patterns.
