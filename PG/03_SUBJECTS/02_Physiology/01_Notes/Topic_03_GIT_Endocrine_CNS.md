# GIT, Endocrine, CNS, And Reproductive Physiology

## GIT
- Salivary, gastric, pancreatic, biliary secretion and intestinal motility.
- Hormones, digestion, absorption, and liver functions.

## Endocrine And Reproductive
- Pituitary, thyroid, adrenal, pancreas, calcium metabolism, puberty, menstrual cycle, pregnancy.
- Hormone action and second messenger linkage are common MCQ points.

## CNS And Special Senses
- Sleep, memory, pain pathways, basal ganglia, cerebellum, autonomic nervous system.
- Vision, hearing, vestibular physiology, and reflex arcs.

## Must Learn

- Menstrual cycle hormonal changes.
- Sleep stages.
- Cerebellar vs basal ganglia lesions.
