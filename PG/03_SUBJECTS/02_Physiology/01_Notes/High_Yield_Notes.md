# Physiology High-Yield Notes

## General And Cellular Physiology
- Resting membrane potential, action potential, transport mechanisms, and body fluid compartments.
- Receptor types, second messengers, and feedback control loops are core basics.

## Nerve, Muscle, And Blood
- Neuromuscular junction, excitation-contraction coupling, and muscle fiber types.
- Hemostasis, coagulation pathways, anemia basics, and blood group systems.

## Cardiovascular System
- Cardiac cycle, heart sounds, pressure-volume loop, cardiac output regulation.
- Baroreceptor reflex, venous return, shock physiology, and ECG basics.

## Respiratory System
- Lung volumes, diffusion, V/Q mismatch, oxygen dissociation curve, and dead space.
- Compliance, surfactant, regulation of breathing, and hypoxia types.

## Renal And Acid-Base
- GFR regulation, tubular transport, countercurrent mechanism, and RAAS.
- Metabolic vs respiratory acidosis/alkalosis and compensation patterns.

## Gastrointestinal And Endocrine
- Gastric secretion, pancreatic enzymes, bile salts, and motility.
- Pituitary, thyroid, adrenal, pancreas, calcium regulation, and menstrual physiology.

## CNS And Special Senses
- Sleep, memory, basal ganglia, cerebellum, hypothalamus, and pain pathways.
- Vision pathway, hearing physiology, vestibular apparatus, and reflexes.
