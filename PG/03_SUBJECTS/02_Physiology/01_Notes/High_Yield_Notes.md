# Physiology High-Yield Notes

Use this file as the main physiology dashboard for NEET PG.

## What Physiology Gives You In NEET PG
- Direct concept MCQs from CVS, respiratory, renal, endocrine, and CNS physiology.
- Graph and curve questions.
- Normal value and formula questions.
- Integrated interpretation: ECG, ABG, lung volumes, acid-base.

## Highest Yield Areas
- Cardiac cycle and pressure-volume loop.
- ECG, heart sounds, and cardiac output control.
- Oxygen dissociation curve, lung volumes, V/Q mismatch.
- GFR, clearance, tubular physiology, acid-base.
- Endocrine axes and hormone receptor-second messenger links.
- Menstrual cycle and reproductive physiology.
- Sleep, basal ganglia, cerebellum, and reflexes.

## How To Study
- Learn physiology through mechanism, not memorized statements.
- Keep one page of formulas and one page of normal values.
- Practice graphs as pattern-recognition questions.

## Must-Memorize Lists
- Hormones and second messengers.
- Acid-base compensation rules.
- Lung volumes and capacities.
- Clearance formulas.
- Cardiac cycle events with ECG/heart sound correlation.
