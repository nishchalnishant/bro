# Physiology One-Liners For Final Revision

- SA node has fastest normal pacemaker rate.
- End-diastolic volume reflects preload.
- Carbon monoxide causes left shift of oxygen dissociation curve.
- Type I respiratory failure is hypoxemic.
- Para-aminohippurate approximates renal plasma flow.
- Aldosterone increases sodium reabsorption and potassium secretion.
- Insulin receptor is tyrosine kinase.
- ACTH, FSH, LH, TSH use cAMP.
- Slow-wave sleep is deepest restorative sleep.
