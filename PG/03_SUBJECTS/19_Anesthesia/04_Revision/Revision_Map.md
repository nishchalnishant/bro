# Anesthesia Revision Map

## First Revision
- Airway assessment, GA agents, spinal anesthesia, and local anesthetic toxicity.

## Second Revision
- Monitoring, ventilator basics, muscle relaxants, and malignant hyperthermia.

## Final Revision
- CPR, emergency drugs, and instrument recognition.

## Must Recall Lists
- Difficult airway predictors.
- Spinal anesthesia complications.
- Local anesthetic maximum doses.
- Reversal agents and indications.
