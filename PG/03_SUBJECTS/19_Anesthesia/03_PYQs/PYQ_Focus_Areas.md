# Anesthesia PYQ Focus Areas

## Repeated PYQ Themes
- Mallampati and difficult airway.
- Spinal anesthesia complications.
- Muscle relaxants and malignant hyperthermia.
- CPR and local anesthetic toxicity.

## PYQ Workflow
- Separate airway PYQs from pharmacology and regional anesthesia PYQs.
- Maintain a repeat list for emergency responses.
