# Anesthesia High-Yield Notes

## Preoperative And Monitoring
- Pre-anesthetic evaluation, ASA grading, airway assessment, fasting, and consent.
- Monitoring includes ECG, pulse oximetry, capnography, and blood pressure.

## General And Regional Anesthesia
- Stages of anesthesia, inhalational agents, IV agents, and common complications.
- Spinal, epidural, nerve blocks, and contraindications are core topics.

## Airway And Ventilation
- Laryngoscope, ET tube, LMA, difficult airway, intubation sequence, and ventilator basics.

## Pharmacology And Emergencies
- Local anesthetics, malignant hyperthermia, muscle relaxants, reversal agents, and opioid use.
- CPR algorithms and perioperative emergency management are high yield.

## Pain And ICU Basics
- Postoperative pain control, PCA, sedation, and shock support basics.
