# Anesthesia MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Airway and difficult intubation.
- GA agents and complications.
- Spinal/epidural anesthesia.
- Local anesthetic toxicity and CPR.

## Practice Method
- Solve airway and drug questions separately.
- Repeat emergency anesthesia MCQs weekly.
- Keep one sheet for monitoring and reversal agents.

- First pass: 58%+
- Second pass: 74%+
- Final phase: 84%+
