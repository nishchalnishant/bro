# Biochemistry High-Yield Notes

## Biomolecules And Enzymes
- Protein structure, enzyme kinetics, inhibition types, and collagen disorders.
- Vitamins, cofactors, and mineral deficiency states are high yield.

## Molecular Biology
- DNA replication, transcription, translation, mutations, and repair mechanisms.
- PCR, blotting techniques, and gene regulation are repeatedly asked.

## Carbohydrate Metabolism
- Glycolysis, TCA cycle, glycogen metabolism, HMP shunt, and gluconeogenesis.
- Rate-limiting enzymes and inborn errors should be memorized.

## Lipid And Protein Metabolism
- Beta oxidation, ketogenesis, fatty acid synthesis, lipoproteins, and cholesterol pathway.
- Urea cycle, transamination, and amino acid disorders are essential.

## Heme, Nucleotides, And Genetics
- Heme synthesis, porphyrias, bilirubin metabolism, purine/pyrimidine synthesis.
- Lesch-Nyhan, gout, folate trap, and inheritance patterns are classic.

## Clinical Biochemistry
- Tumor markers, liver function tests, renal markers, and nutrition markers.
- Metabolic integration during fasting, fed state, and starvation.
