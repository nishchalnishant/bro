# Vitamins, Enzymes, And One-Liners

- Thiamine deficiency:
  beriberi, Wernicke-Korsakoff.
- Niacin deficiency:
  pellagra.
- Vitamin C deficiency:
  scurvy.
- B12 deficiency:
  methylmalonic acid increases.
- Folate deficiency does not raise methylmalonic acid.
- HGPRT deficiency causes Lesch-Nyhan syndrome.
