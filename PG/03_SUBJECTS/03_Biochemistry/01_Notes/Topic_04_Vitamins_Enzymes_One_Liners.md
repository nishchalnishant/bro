## 2. Rate-Limiting Enzymes (High Yield List)
- **Heme Synthesis**: ALA Synthase.
- **Urea Cycle**: CPS-1.
- **Lipogenesis**: Acetyl-CoA Carboxylase.
- **Bile Acid Synthesis**: 7-alpha-hydroxylase.
- **Ketogenesis**: HMG-CoA Synthase.
- **Purine Synthesis**: PRPP Glutamyl Amidotransferase.
- **Pyrimidine Synthesis**: CPS-2.

## 3. Rapid-Recall One-Liners
1. **Collagen Synthesis**: Requires Vitamin C for hydroxylation.
2. **Homocysteine to Methionine**: Requires B12 and Folate.
3. **Propionyl-CoA to Methylmalonyl-CoA**: Requires Biotin.
4. **Methylmalonyl-CoA to Succinyl-CoA**: Requires B12.
5. **G6PD Deficiency**: X-linked; Heinz bodies, Bite cells.
6. **Albinism**: Tyrosinase deficiency.
7. **Lesch-Nyhan Syndrome**: HGPRT deficiency (Hyperuricemia, Self-mutilation).
8. **Glucose-Alanine Cycle**: Transports Nitrogen from muscle to liver.
9. **Cori Cycle**: Transports Lactate from muscle to liver.
10. **Zellweger Syndrome**: Peroxisome biogenesis defect (Long chain fatty acids).
11. **Refsum Disease**: Phytenic acid alpha-oxidation defect.
12. **Niacin Synthesis**: Derived from **Tryptophan** (Requires B2, B6).
13. **Selenium**: Essential for Glutathione Peroxidase.
14. **Zinc**: Essential for Alcohol Dehydrogenase.
15. **Copper**: Essential for Cytochrome c Oxidase and Lysyl Oxidase.
