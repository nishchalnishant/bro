# Carbohydrate, Lipid, And Protein Metabolism

## Carbohydrates
- Glycolysis, gluconeogenesis, glycogen metabolism, HMP shunt, and TCA cycle.
- Rate-limiting enzymes and regulatory hormones should be memorized.

## Lipids
- Fatty acid oxidation, synthesis, ketone bodies, cholesterol pathway, and lipoproteins.
- Clinical dyslipidemia patterns and apoproteins are common MCQs.

## Proteins
- Transamination, deamination, urea cycle, amino acid metabolism, and related disorders.

## Must Learn
- Glycogen storage diseases.
- Lipoprotein functions.
- Urea cycle defects.
- Fed-fast-state integration.
