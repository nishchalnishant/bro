# Carbohydrate, Lipid, And Protein Metabolism

## 1. Carbohydrate Metabolism

### Energy Yield Summary
- **Glycolysis (Aerobic)**: 5 or 7 ATP (Malate-Aspartate or Glycerol-P shuttle).
- **Glycolysis (Anaerobic)**: 2 ATP.
- **TCA Cycle**: 10 ATP per Acetyl-CoA (20 per Glucose).
- **Complete Oxidation of Glucose**: 30 or 32 ATP.

### Glycogen Storage Diseases (GSDs) - (High Yield)
| Type | Name | Deficient Enzyme | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Type I** | **Von Gierke** | Glucose-6-Phosphatase | Severe fast hypoglycemia, Lactic acidosis |
| **Type II** | **Pompe** | Lysosomal alpha-glucosidase | **Cardiomegaly**, Muscle weakness |
| **Type III** | **Cori** | Debranching enzyme | Normal lactate |
| **Type V** | **McArdle** | Muscle Phosphorylase | Muscle cramps during exercise |

- **Galactosemia**: Deficiency of Galactose-1-phosphate uridyltransferase (GALT) -> Oil drop cataract.

## 2. Lipid Metabolism

### Beta-Oxidation
- **Location**: Mitochondria.
- **Carnitine Shuttle**: Rate-limiting transport of FA into mitochondria.
- **Energy Yield (Palmitate 16C)**: 106 ATP.

### Lipoproteins & Apoproteins
- **Chylomicrons**: Carry dietary TG. (Apo B48).
- **VLDL**: Carry hepatic TG. (Apo B100).
- **LDL**: Carry Cholesterol. (Apo B100). **Bad Cholesterol**.
- **HDL**: Reverse cholesterol transport. (Apo A-I). **Good Cholesterol**.
- **Lp(a)**: Structural analog of Plasminogen; independent risk factor for CAD.

## 3. Protein & Amino Acid Metabolism

### Urea Cycle
- **Location**: Partially Mitochondrial, partially Cytosolic.
- **Rate Limiting Step**: **CPS-1** (requires N-acetylglutamate).
- **Most Common Defect**: **Ornithine Transcarbamoylase (OTC) deficiency** (X-linked).

### Inborn Errors of Metabolism (IEM)
| Disorder | Deficient Enzyme | Key Sign / Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PKU** | Phenylalanine Hydroxylase | **Musty odor**, Intellectual disability |
| **Alkaptonuria** | Homogentisate Oxidase | **Black urine** (on standing), Ochronosis |
| **MSUD** | BCKAD complex | **Maple syrup odor** of urine |
| **Homocystinuria**| Cystathionine Synthase | Ectopia lentis (Downward), Marfanoid habitus |

## 4. Rate-Limiting Enzymes (Master Summary)
- **Glycolysis**: PFK-1.
- **Gluconeogenesis**: Fructose-1,6-bisphosphatase.
- **TCA Cycle**: Isocitrate Dehydrogenase.
- **Glycogenesis**: Glycogen Synthase.
- **Glycogenolysis**: Glycogen Phosphorylase.
- **FA Synthesis**: Acetyl-CoA Carboxylase.
- **Cholesterol Synthesis**: HMG-CoA Reductase.
