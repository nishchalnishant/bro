# Pathology One-Liners For Final Revision

- Liquefactive necrosis is classically seen in brain infarct.
- Granuloma has epithelioid histiocytes.
- p53 is tumor suppressor gene.
- CML is associated with BCR-ABL.
- Reed-Sternberg is CD15 and CD30 positive classically.
- Minimal change disease causes nephrotic syndrome.
