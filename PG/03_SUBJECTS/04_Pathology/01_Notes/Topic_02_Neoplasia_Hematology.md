# Neoplasia And Hematology

## Neoplasia
- Benign vs malignant features, carcinogenesis, oncogenes, tumor suppressors, metastasis.
- Tumor markers, grading, staging, and hallmark features are central.

## Hematology
- Iron deficiency, megaloblastic anemia, hemolytic anemia, hemoglobinopathies, aplastic anemia.
- Leukemias, lymphomas, plasma cell dyscrasias, and myeloproliferative neoplasms.

## Must Learn
- Tumor suppressor genes.
- FAB/WHO style leukemia patterns.
- Lymphoma markers.
- Anemia lab differentiation.
