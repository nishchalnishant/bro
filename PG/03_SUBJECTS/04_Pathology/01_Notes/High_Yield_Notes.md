# Pathology High-Yield Notes

## General Pathology
- Cell injury, apoptosis, necrosis, inflammation, repair, and hemodynamic disorders.
- Edema, thrombosis, embolism, infarction, shock, and amyloidosis are staples.

## Neoplasia
- Hallmarks of cancer, oncogenes, tumor suppressors, metastasis, grading, and staging.
- Tumor markers and paraneoplastic syndromes are frequent MCQ areas.

## Hematology
- Iron deficiency, megaloblastic anemia, hemolytic anemia, thalassemia, and aplastic anemia.
- Leukemias, lymphomas, myeloproliferative disorders, and plasma cell disorders.

## Systemic Pathology
- CVS: rheumatic fever, infective endocarditis, MI, and vasculitis.
- Respiratory: pneumonia, TB, COPD, asthma, and lung cancers.
- GIT, liver, kidney, endocrine, breast, CNS, bone, and skin pathology.

## Female, Male, And Breast Pathology
- CIN, cervical cancer, endometrial hyperplasia, ovarian tumors, BPH, and prostate carcinoma.
- Fibroadenoma, fibrocystic change, and breast carcinoma markers.

## Image And Slide Focus
- Gross specimens, histology patterns, cell inclusions, and cytology clues.
