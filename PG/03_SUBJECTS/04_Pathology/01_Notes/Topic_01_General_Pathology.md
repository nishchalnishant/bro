# General Pathology

## Cell Injury And Adaptation
- Reversible injury, irreversible injury, apoptosis, necrosis, and intracellular accumulations.
- Adaptation includes hypertrophy, hyperplasia, atrophy, and metaplasia.

## Inflammation And Repair
- Acute inflammation mediators, chronic inflammation cells, granuloma, and wound healing.
- Tissue repair, fibrosis, and factors affecting wound healing are important.

## Hemodynamic Disorders
- Edema, thrombosis, embolism, infarction, shock, and DIC.

## Must Learn
- Necrosis types.
- Granuloma causes.
- Shock stages.
- Healing by primary vs secondary intention.
