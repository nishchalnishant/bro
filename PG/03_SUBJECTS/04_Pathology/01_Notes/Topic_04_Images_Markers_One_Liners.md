## 2. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) Marker Master Bank
- **Cytokeratin (CK)**: Epithelial tumors (Carcinomas).
- **Vimentin**: Mesenchymal tumors (Sarcomas).
- **Desmin**: Muscle tumors (Leiomyoma/Rhabdomyoma).
- **S100**: Melanoma, Schwannoma, Neural crest tumors.
- **HMB-45**: Melanoma specific.
- **PSA**: Prostate cancer.
- **Thyroglobulin**: Thyroid cancer.
- **Calretinin**: Mesothelioma.
- **Chromogranin / Synaptophysin**: Neuroendocrine tumors (Small cell lung, Carcinoid).
- **CD117 (c-kit)**: Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor (GIST).

## 3. High-Yield Translocations (The 4-Star List)
- **t(8;14)**: Burkitt Lymphoma (c-MYC).
- **t(14;18)**: Follicular Lymphoma (Bcl-2).
- **t(11;14)**: Mantle Cell Lymphoma (Cyclin D1).
- **t(9;22)**: CML / ALL (BCR-ABL - Philadelphia).
- **t(15;17)**: APL - AML M3 (PML-RARA).
- **t(11;22)**: Ewing Sarcoma.
- **t(X;18)**: Synovial Sarcoma.

## 4. Rapid-Recall Pathological Signs
1. **Starry Sky Appearance**: Burkitt Lymphoma.
2. **Fish-flesh tumor**: Lymphomas / Sarcomas.
3. **Orphan Annie Eye**: Papillary Thyroid Carcinoma.
4. **Homer Wright Rosettes**: Neuroblastoma / Medulloblastoma.
5. **Flexner-Wintersteiner**: Retinoblastoma.
6. **Fried-egg appearance**: Oligodendroglioma / Seminoma.
7. **School of fish**: Haemophilus ducreyi (Chancroid).
8. **String sign of Kantor**: Crohn's disease.
9. **Lead-pipe colon**: Ulcerative Colitis.
10. **Apple-core lesion**: Colorectal Cancer (Left-sided).
