# Systemic Pathology And Images

## Systemic Pathology
- Heart, lung, liver, kidney, breast, endocrine, CNS, skin, bone, and GIT pathology.
- Focus on morphology-disease associations and classic image findings.

## Image-Based Pathology
- Gross specimens, histology slides, cytology findings, and inclusion bodies.
- Match tumors and diseases with their signature microscopic appearances.
