# Pathology Last Minute Revision Sheet: Pathology

Rapid recall for the final 48 hours.

## 1. Amyloidosis Typing (Very High Yield)
- **AL**: Primary; Light chain (Plasma cells).
- **AA**: Secondary; SAA protein (Chronic inflammation).
- **A-beta**: Alzheimer's.
- **ATTR**: Transthyretin (Senile/Familial).
- **Stain**: Congo Red -> Apple-green birefringence.

## 2. Oncology Master Genes
| Gene | Type | Associated Tumor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RB** | Governor | Retinoblastoma, Osteosarcoma |
| **p53** | Guardian | Most common cancer gene |
| **MYC** | Oncogene | Burkitt Lymphoma |
| **RET** | Oncogene | MEN 2A/2B, Medullary Thyroid Ca |
| **WT-1** | Suppressor | Wilms Tumor |

## 3. The "Master List of Bodies" (Recap)
- **Psammoma**: Laminated calcium (Papillary Thyroid Ca).
- **Aschoff**: Caterpillar cells (Rheumatic Heart Disease).
- **Councilman**: Apoptotic hepatocytes (Viral Hepatitis).
- **Mallory-Denk**: Ubiquitinated keratin (Alcoholic Liver).
- **Call-Exner**: Rosettes (Granulosa Cell).

## 4. IHC Marker Checklist
- **CK+**: Epithelial tumors.
- **Vimentin+**: Mesenchymal tumors.
- **S100+**: Melanoma / Neural.
- **HMB-45**: Melanoma specific.
- **Calretinin**: Mesothelioma.
