# Image And Table Checklist: Pathology

Pathology is the #1 subject for IBQs (Image Based Questions). Master these "One-Look" features.

## 1. Neoplasia & General Pathology
1. **Starry Sky Pattern**: Burkitt Lymphoma (Macrophages eating apoptotic cells).
2. **Keratin Pearls**: Squamous Cell Carcinoma.
3. **Orphan Annie Eye Nuclei**: Papillary Thyroid Carcinoma.
4. **Schiller-Duval Bodies**: Yolk Sac Tumor (Glomeruloid).
5. **Call-Exner Bodies**: Granulosa Cell Tumor (Rosettes).
6. **Fried-egg appearance**: Dysgerminoma / Seminoma.
7. **Psammoma Bodies**: Laminated calcification (Papillary Ca / Meningioma).
8. **Homer Wright Rosettes**: Neuroblastoma / Medulloblastoma.
9. **Flexner-Wintersteiner Rosettes**: Retinoblastoma.
10. **Azzopardi Effect**: Small cell carcinoma lung.

## 2. Hematology Smears
11. **Hypersegmented Neutrophils**: Megaloblastic Anemia (B12/Folate).
12. **Bite Cells / Heinz Bodies**: G6PD Deficiency.
13. **Schistocytes (Helmet cells)**: Microangiopathic Hemolytic Anemia (TTP/HUS/DIC).
14. **Target Cells**: Thalassemia / Liver Disease.
15. **Auer Rods**: AML (especially M3).
16. **Smudge Cells**: CLL.
17. **Reed-Sternberg Cells**: Hodgkin Lymphoma.
18. **Pelger-Huet Anomaly**: Dumbbell-shaped neutrophils (MDS).
19. **Rouleaux Formation**: Multiple Myeloma.
20. **Ring Sideroblasts**: Sideroblastic Anemia (Iron in mitochondria).

## 3. Systemic Pathology (Gross & Micro)
21. **Aschoff Bodies**: Rheumatic Heart Disease.
22. **Anitschkow Cells**: "Caterpillar nuclei" in RHD.
23. **Vegetations on both sides of valves**: Libman-Sacks (SLE).
24. **Nutmeg Liver**: Chronic Venous Congestion.
25. **Mallory-Denk Bodies**: Alcoholic Liver Disease.
26. **Ground-glass Hepatocytes**: Hepatitis B (HBsAg accumulation).
27. **Councilman Bodies**: Viral Hepatitis (Apoptosis).
28. **Kimmelstiel-Wilson Nodules**: Diabetic Nephropathy.
29. **Subepithelial Humps (EM)**: PSGN.
30. **Spike and Dome (Silver stain)**: Membranous Nephropathy.
31. **Tram-track appearance (Silver stain)**: MPGN.
32. **Crescents in Glomerulus**: RPGN.
33. **Barrett's Esophagus**: Columnar metaplasia with Goblet cells.
34. **Signet Ring Cells**: Gastric Adenocarcinoma (Diffuse type).
35. **Codman's Triangle / Sunburst**: Osteosarcoma.
36. **Onion Skinning (Bone X-ray)**: Ewing Sarcoma.
37. **Soap Bubble Appearance (X-ray)**: Giant Cell Tumor of bone.
38. **Birbeck Granules (EM)**: "Tennis racket" in Langerhans Cell Histiocytosis.
39. **Negri Bodies**: Rabies (Intracytoplasmic).
40. **Lewy Bodies**: Parkinson's (Intracytoplasmic Alpha-synuclein).
41. **Hirano Bodies**: Alzheimer's.
42. **Neurofibrillary Tangles**: Alzheimer's (Hyperphosphorylated Tau).
43. **Koilocytes**: HPV infection (Cervix).
44. **Charcot-Leyden Crystals**: Asthma (Eosinophil breakdown).
45. **Curschmann Spirals**: Asthma (Mucus plugs).
46. **H-shaped vertebrae**: Sickle Cell Disease.
47. **Crew-cut appearance (Skull X-ray)**: Thalassemia / Sickle Cell.
48. **Apple-core lesion (Barium)**: Colorectal Ca.
49. **String sign of Kantor**: Crohn's disease.
50. **Lead pipe colon**: Ulcerative Colitis.
