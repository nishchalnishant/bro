# Injuries And Asphyxia

## Mechanical Injuries
- Abrasion, bruise, laceration, incised wounds, stab wounds, firearm injuries, and defense wounds.

## Asphyxial Deaths
- Hanging, strangulation, drowning, smothering, choking, and traumatic asphyxia.
- Differentiate findings by ligature mark, hyoid fracture, and scene clues.

## Must Learn
- Injury-weapon correlation.
- Hanging vs strangulation.
- Drowning signs.
- Firearm wound features.
