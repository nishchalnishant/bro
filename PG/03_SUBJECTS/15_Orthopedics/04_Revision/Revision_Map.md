# Orthopedics Revision Map

- Fracture complications, common dislocations, nerve injuries, and spine lesions.

## Second Revision
- Bone tumors, infections, pediatric orthopedics, and arthritis tables.

- X-ray based diagnosis, implants, and traction-splint instruments.

- Bone tumor age and site.
