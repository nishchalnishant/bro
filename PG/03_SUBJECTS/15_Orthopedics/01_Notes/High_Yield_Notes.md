# Orthopedics High-Yield Notes

## Bone And Fracture Basics
- Bone healing, fracture types, complications, and principles of immobilization.
- Open fracture grading and nonunion causes are important.

## Trauma And Upper/Lower Limb
- Shoulder dislocation, supracondylar fracture, Colles fracture, neck of femur, and hip dislocation.
- Common nerve injuries associated with fractures are frequently asked.

## Spine, Arthritis, And Infection
- Disc prolapse, spondylolisthesis, TB spine, osteomyelitis, septic arthritis, and osteoarthritis.

## Bone Tumors And Pediatric Ortho
- Osteosarcoma, Ewing sarcoma, giant cell tumor, SCFE, DDH, clubfoot, and Perthes disease.

## Instruments And Imaging
- Traction types, splints, casts, X-ray signs, and orthopedic implants.
