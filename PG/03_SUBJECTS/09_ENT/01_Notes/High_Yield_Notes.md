# ENT High-Yield Notes

## Ear
- External, middle, and inner ear anatomy with hearing physiology.
- Otitis media, cholesteatoma, facial nerve palsy, and mastoid complications.

## Audiology And Vertigo
- Rinne, Weber, ABC, audiogram interpretation, Meniere disease, and BPPV.

## Nose And PNS
- DNS, epistaxis, nasal polyps, sinusitis, fungal sinusitis, and CSF rhinorrhea.

## Pharynx And Larynx
- Tonsillitis, peritonsillar abscess, adenoids, laryngeal cancers, vocal cord palsy.
- Airway emergencies and tracheostomy basics are important.

## Head And Neck
- Deep neck spaces, salivary gland disorders, thyroid swelling basics, and neck nodes.
- Instruments, endoscopic views, and image-based questions are high yield.
