# Medicine High-Yield Notes

## Cardiology
- ACS, heart failure, valvular disease, arrhythmias, infective endocarditis, and hypertension.
- ECG interpretation, murmur clues, and shock classification are core.

## Respiratory And Critical Care
- Asthma, COPD, pneumonia, ARDS, pleural effusion, pulmonary embolism, and oxygen therapy.
- ABG interpretation and acid-base disorders are repeatedly asked.

## Gastroenterology And Hepatology
- GI bleed, pancreatitis, hepatitis, cirrhosis, portal hypertension, and IBD.
- LFT patterns and complications of chronic liver disease are high yield.

## Nephrology And Electrolytes
- AKI, CKD, nephritic/nephrotic syndromes, dialysis basics, sodium and potassium disorders.

## Neurology, Endocrine, And Rheumatology
- Stroke, seizures, meningitis, neuropathy, thyroid disease, diabetes, DKA, HHS, adrenal disorders.
- SLE, RA, vasculitis, and gout are classic integrated topics.

## Hematology, Infectious Diseases, And Poisoning
- Anemia workup, leukemia overview, HIV, malaria, dengue, sepsis, and common poisonings.
