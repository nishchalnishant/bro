# Cardiology, Respiratory, And Nephrology

## Cardiology
- ACS, heart failure, valvular lesions, hypertension, cardiomyopathy, arrhythmias, and shock.
- ECG interpretation and murmur-based diagnosis are central.

## Respiratory
- Asthma, COPD, pneumonia, pleural effusion, pulmonary embolism, and ARDS.
- Correlate ABG with respiratory pathology.

## Nephrology
- AKI, CKD, nephritic and nephrotic syndrome, electrolyte disorders, and dialysis basics.

## Must Learn
- ECG approach.
- Murmur clues.
- ABG patterns.
- Sodium and potassium disorder management.
