# Ophthalmology PYQ Focus Areas

## Repeated PYQ Themes
- Open-angle vs angle-closure glaucoma.
- Cataract types and lens abnormalities.
- Retinal vascular occlusions and fundus clues.
- Visual field defects and cranial nerve lesions.

## PYQ Workflow
- Keep a separate list for fundus images and visual field questions.
- Revisit red eye and leukocoria PYQs often.
