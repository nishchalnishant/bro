# Respiratory Medicine MCQ Strategy And Topics

## Priority MCQ Zones
- Obstructive vs restrictive disease.
- TB diagnosis and treatment.
- Pleural disease and pneumothorax.
- PFT, oxygen therapy, and ABG.

## Practice Method
- Solve PFT and ABG questions repeatedly in mixed sets.
- Keep one list for TB drug adverse effects and pleural approach.
- Repeat ventilation/NIV questions weekly.

## Accuracy Targets
- First pass: 58%+
- Second pass: 74%+
- Final phase: 84%+
