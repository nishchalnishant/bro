# Obstructive, Restrictive, And PFT

## Obstructive Disease
- Asthma, COPD, bronchiectasis, bronchiolitis, and airflow limitation patterns.

## Restrictive Disease
- ILD, pneumoconiosis, sarcoidosis, and chest wall/neuromuscular restriction.

## PFT
- FEV1, FVC, FEV1/FVC, DLCO, and pattern recognition.

## Must Learn
- Obstructive vs restrictive PFT.
- Occupational lung exposures.
- Asthma severity.
- COPD staging basics.
